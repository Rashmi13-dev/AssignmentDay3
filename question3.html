Diet: Eating a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains ensures you get the necessary nutrients.

Exercise: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week, along with muscle-strengthening exercises.

Hydration: Drinking enough water is crucial for maintaining bodily functions and energy levels. Aim for about 8 glasses a day, but individual needs can vary.

Quality Sleep: Try to get 7-9 hours of sleep per night to support overall health, mood, and cognitive function.

Stress Management: Techniques such as mindfulness, meditation, or yoga can help manage stress and improve mental well-being.
